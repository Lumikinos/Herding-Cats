"Herding Cats", a simple in-browser game. Objective: Use dog sprite to herd cats into a box while avoiding the traps. Level Progression: Positive points are awarded for cats reaching the box, and points are taken away for cats being trapped. When all cats have been herded or trapped, the playing field is reset with additional traps. End State: The game ends when the player has negative points. The final score is the highest level reached by the player.
